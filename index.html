The majestic ambience and massive bar with a mother-of-pearl finish set Prequel apart from other resto-bars in the city. This vast industrial-chic space, under AA Hospitality, is adorned with tasteful paper lamps, rattan chairs and lots of leafy plants. The folks behind Brassa, Misu and Lucky Chan have put together an expansive menu of food and cocktails that offer a unique twist on familiar flavours. Think raw mango and peanut chicken tikka or poha crusted fish fingers. Look forward to a range of European, Middle-Eastern and pan-Asian cuisines, and sample Indian grills, sushi, dim sums, as well as their distinctive sundal hummus.
